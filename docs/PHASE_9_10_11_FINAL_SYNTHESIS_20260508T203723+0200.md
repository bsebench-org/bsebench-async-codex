## Synthesis

The completion audit falsifies Phase 9/10/11 scientific completion. The product
repositories contain substantial mechanical preparation: schemas, contracts,
gates, dry-run manifests, budgets, smoke tests, and non-slow validation trails.
Those artifacts are useful and should be preserved as readiness infrastructure.

The same evidence does not close any of the three phases. The missing layer is
empirical, source-ledger-backed, and verdict-level evidence: frozen run
artifacts, traceable commands, dataset/cache/provenance readiness, real
external-source rows where comparisons are attempted, BSEBench frozen value
rows, comparison bindings, and final scientific verdict documents.

The three final-verdict branches visible to this worker do not add independent
verdict files. That is a blocker for treating them as phase evidence. The only
direct Phase 9/10/11 audit artifact read here is the completion audit, and it
explicitly concludes that Phase 9/10/11 are mechanically prepared but not
scientifically complete.
